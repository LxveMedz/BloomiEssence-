Our Story

Blooming From a Better Idea

Working in the cleaning industry, my partner and I were consistently exposed to conventional cleaning products on a daily basis.

Over time, the harsh chemicals began taking a toll on us — causing headaches, nausea, and even making our noses feel raw.

After a while of thinking, planning, and experimenting with different botanical ingredients, my partner and I decided to create a natural cleaning solution — not only for ourselves, but for the environment and the people we care for.

That decision led us to begin developing our product with a more mindful, refreshing, and beneficial approach to cleaning.

Essentially, blooming this idea into reality.
