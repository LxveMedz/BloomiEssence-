What's Inside

Eight ingredients.
Nothing hidden.

01
💧

Distilled Water

The pure base of every formula. Mineral-free so it won't leave residue or interfere with the active ingredients.

02
🍋

Citric Acid

A naturally derived acid that breaks down mineral deposits, soap scum, and grime while balancing the formula's pH.

03
🌿

TSGD

A plant-derived surfactant that boosts cleaning performance and helps lift dirt and oils from surfaces effectively.

04
🔬

Sodium Gluconate

A biodegradable chelating agent that softens water and helps the other ingredients work more efficiently on tough buildup.

05
🫧

Decyl Glucoside

A gentle, plant-based surfactant derived from corn and coconut. Breaks down grease and dirt without harsh chemicals.

06
🌱

Witch Hazel

A botanical astringent that boosts cleaning power, reduces bacteria, and helps the formula dry quickly and streak-free.

07
🌸

Essential Oil

Adds a natural fragrance and extra antimicrobial strength. Clean should smell as good as it works.

08
🛡️

Geogard 221

A globally approved, biodegradable preservative system that keeps the formula fresh and effective from first use to last.
